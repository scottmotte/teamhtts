About HTTS

Learn who we are and why we do this

HTTS, or Humberto’s Training and Technical Support was born from the realization that the Hino dealerships in Southern California were lacking. This was discovered through the experiences of both a customer and a technician. Dan Sparks was a customer that had numerous disappointing experiences with dealerships regarding his freight company in Southern California. Dan began to see that there was a significant element missing in every transaction- customer service. The Hino product had proven itself to be superior to the other brands, but there were not adequate representatives that could professionally handle the Hino customers. He voiced his opinion of the need for a better training facility that equaled the high quality standard of the Hino brand to his colleague, Humberto Castellanos, and they formed HTTS as an answer to the problem.

Humberto's Training and Technical Support is, at its core, a school for diesel mechanics - online and in person- teaching service, parts, and sales to individuals. It offers the opportunity for students to exclusively learn how to maintain Hino trucks, build relationships with clients, and work in a professional setting. HTTS is training the next generation of great Hino mechanics. The target audience for HTTS is any individual seeking to become an extraordinary Hino mechanic or any franchise owner looking to train their own employees on our high-caliber system. Our students will come from all over the world and once they complete our training, they are a part of our family- continuously adding to our support system over time.
